The insurance companies with which I am contracted at this time are Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, and HealthLink.Please print out and complete the Insurance Information Form if you plan to pay using one of those benefits.

I do not file insurance forms for any other insurers, but I can provide you with a monthly statement outlining dates of sessions, charges and paid fees. To find out what your out-of-network benefits are, you will need to contact your insurance company. I have included a helpful guide titled, Health Insurance Out of Network to assist you in this process. My fee is $100 per 50-55 minute session. Payment is due at the time of your session and can be made by cash, credit card, or check.

If you plan to pay for your sessions out of pocket, please complete the Private Payment Agreement.

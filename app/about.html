Sarah Strohmayer

MA, LPC, NCC

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of Missouri and a Nationally Certified Counselor. I have a B.A. in Psychology from the University of Missouri-Columbia and an M.A. in School and Professional Counseling from Lindenwood University.

I am dedicated to helping people gain control of their life and assist in making changes to strive to become the person they desire to be. I provide a secure and nurturing environment for clients to explore deep into themselves and uncover past experiences or beliefs that could be hindering growth.

I have experience working with adults in private practice and adolescents both in private practice and as a school counselor.

I work with clients on many issues including:

  • Anxiety
  • Stress
  • Depression
  • Self-Harm
  • Grief
  • Trauma
  • Suicidal Ideation/Behaviors
  • Obsessive-Compulsive (OCD)
  • Relationship Problems
  • Peer Relationships
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Divorce
  • Assertiveness
  • Life Coaching
  • Self-Esteem
  • Shyness

The population I work with includes adolescents, young adults, and adults in individual therapy. My treatment modalities include Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy, Mindfulness-based, Motivational Interviewing, EMDR (Eye-Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), and Relaxation Training.
